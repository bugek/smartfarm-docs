# Product Vision

SmartFarm is a software platform for agricultural operations that starts with GAP readiness and grows into an advisory and data platform.

## Phase 1

Deliver a working GAP-first operating system for:

- farmers recording work and evidence
- experts reviewing readiness
- organizations preparing audit-ready records

## Future phases

- richer communication channels
- advisor scoring
- recommendation support for fertilizer and crop protection
- AI-assisted case review based on structured field data and media
