# Agent Ownership

## CEO
- approves direction
- sequences the roadmap

## Product GAP Lead
- owns product intent and Phase 1 scope
- maintains `product/`, `gap/` (jointly with GAP Compliance Expert), and the implementation handoff
- single point of contact for changes to the Phase 1 contract

## GAP Compliance Expert
- owns GAP scheme interpretation
- defines the record types, structured fields, and required evidence kinds per scheme
- contributes to `gap/`

## Tech Lead
- owns backend architecture and `smartfarm-api`
- delivers the capabilities listed in `architecture/implementation-handoff.md`
- breaks Phase 1 into engineering child issues

## Platform Engineer
- owns `smartfarm-web`, the primary frontend surface
- delivers the three Phase 1 user flows on mobile browsers
